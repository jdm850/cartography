Mapping California Coastal Fog

Del Norte County

Humboldt County

Sonoma County


Fog is a meteorological phenomenon important to many ecosystems. Fog data is limited and with persistent effects of climate change we risk altering the occurrence and extent of fog development. In order to better understand these fog dependent areas and particular communities which could be impacted by this change, fog extent is visualized here across California coastal regions.
